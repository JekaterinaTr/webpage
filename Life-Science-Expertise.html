Featured Research Projects

Auxin Response Network

How do plants control growth precisely? We discovered a network of repressors that fine-tunes hormone responses.

Goal: Understand how auxin responses are regulated.

Key finding: A network of transcriptional repressors modulates growth signals.

Impact: Improves understanding of plant development control.

Truskina et al. Nature 2021

Anther Development

How do plant tissues communicate during reproduction? We revealed barriers controlling cell-to-cell exchange.

Goal: Study tissue isolation in developing anthers.

Key finding: Symplastic isolation and apoplastic gating regulate development.

Impact: Advances knowledge of reproductive tissue organization.

Truskina et al. Development

Pollen Wall Formation

How do plant cells coordinate to build pollen? We uncovered a peptide-based communication system.

Goal: Understand coordination of pollen wall formation.

Key finding: A peptide-mediated signaling network controls the process.

Impact: Key insight into plant fertility mechanisms.

Truskina et al. PNAS 2022

Plant Immunity & Pathology

How do plants fight pathogens? We studied hormone balance and vascular responses.

Goal: Investigate plant defense mechanisms.

Key finding: Cytokinin stabilization and xylem formation enhance resistance.

Impact: Links immunity with development and stress adaptation.
